What is LoRA/LyCORIS?

LoRA/LyCORIS are models which will make Stable Diffusion generate what you trained them for!

This tutorial will teach you how to collect the dataset required for training LoRA/LyCORIS.

There are two main ways in which you can collect images for dataset.


Way 1 (tradiotional one)

The thing is that you only need 8-30 images to train LoRA of any character choosen by you. That means you can collect images just by searching them with google

Step 1: Find ~12 images of character/artist whose style you want to generate picture with.

Step 2: Using online service Birme crop your images to 512x512 resolution or less if your image was in smaller resolution



Step 3: Create on your desktop folder 100_nameOfLoRA

Step 4: Place your pictures in folder and rename them like on picture below
